Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your information

Information Collection and Use

Personal Information We Collect

When you use our recovery services, we may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Financial information relevant to recovery claims
  • Documentation related to government benefits or payments
  • Communication preferences and history

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide recovery services and match you with detected signals
  • Communicate with government agencies on your behalf
  • Process claims and facilitate recovery procedures
  • Maintain records for compliance and audit purposes
  • Improve our signal detection and recovery systems

Information Sharing

We share information only when:

  • Required for recovery processes with relevant government agencies
  • Necessary for legal compliance or regulatory requirements
  • You provide explicit consent for sharing
  • Required to protect rights, property, or safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted email communications
  • Secure document handling procedures
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Duration of active recovery cases plus 7 years
  • Compliance with government record-keeping requirements
  • Statistical analysis (anonymized data only)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we maintain
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Government Signal Detection

Our signal detection system:

  • Uses only publicly available government data
  • Does not access private or confidential databases
  • Aggregates information to identify patterns and inefficiencies
  • Operates in compliance with data access regulations
