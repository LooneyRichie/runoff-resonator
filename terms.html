Recovery service agreement and terms

Service Agreement

1. Service Description

Runoff Resonator provides recovery services for lost, unclaimed, or misdirected government funds including:

  • Signal detection and matching services
  • Claim preparation and submission assistance
  • Government agency liaison services
  • Recovery facilitation and follow-up
  • Documentation and compliance support

2. Fee Structure

Success-Based Fees:

  • Individual Recovery: 15% of gross recovered amount
  • Government Agency Consultation: Negotiated based on scope
  • No Upfront Costs: Fees only charged upon successful recovery
  • Payment Due: At the time of recovery

3. Client Responsibilities

Clients agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Supply necessary documentation in timely manner
  • Cooperate with recovery processes and government requests
  • Pay agreed fees upon successful recovery
  • Notify us of any changes in contact information

4. Service Limitations

We cannot 100% guarantee:

  • Successful recovery in all cases
  • Specific timelines for government agency responses
  • Recovery of full claimed amounts
  • Expedited processing by government agencies

5. Success Metrics and Expectations

Typical Success Rates:

  • Tax refunds and stimulus payments: 70-80%
  • Benefits overpayments: 60-70%
  • Complex institutional claims: 50-60%
  • Average recovery timeline: Up to 30 days online, 90-180 days by mail

6. Government Compliance

Our services operate in compliance with:

  • Federal records access and FOIA regulations
  • State unclaimed property laws
  • IRS and Treasury Department procedures
  • Professional recovery service standards

7. Confidentiality

We maintain strict confidentiality of:

  • All client personal and financial information
  • Recovery details and claim amounts
  • Government communications and documentation
  • Strategic recovery approaches and methodologies

8. Termination

Either party may terminate services:

  • With 30 days written notice
  • Immediately for breach of agreement
  • Upon completion of recovery services
  • No fees due for work completed before termination

9. Dispute Resolution

Disputes will be resolved through:

  • Good faith negotiation as first step with legal documentation exchange
  • Mediation immediately if negotiation fails
  • Arbitration as final resolution method
  • Governing law: State where client resides

10. Signal Detection Methodology

Our detection system:

  • Monitors public government databases and APIs
  • Identifies patterns indicating potential overpayments or inefficiencies
  • Provides 85-95% confidence matching for individual cases
  • Does not guarantee detection of all possible recovery opportunities

11. Agency Portal Services

For government agencies:

  • 60-day disclosure period before public notification
  • Collaborative recovery approach preferred
  • Technical assistance available on success-fee basis
  • Full transparency and audit trail documentation
